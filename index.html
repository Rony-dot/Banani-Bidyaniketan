About Us



01. Mission
Mission

1. To provide higher education at an affordable cost, keeping in view the economic conditions of the middle and lower middle income families in Bangladesh.
2. To impart world class education.
3. To provide research facilities so that the students can develop their full intellectual, social and personal potentials.
4. To provide knowledge, skills and abilities to transform human resource into human capital.
5. To inculcate moral and ethical values among students.
6. To prepare students to meet the challenges of the 21st century.

02. Vision
Vision

The Vision of Southeast University is: To Obtain Global Recognition as a World Class University.
